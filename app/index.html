WE STILL ARE RAILFANS

Railfan 25th Convention

February 28 2015

Information


RAILFAN is proud to announce the 25th RailFan Convention, in which train enthusiasts gather together to share their experiences with other fans while making a delightful and enjoyable train trip through entertaining routes. This year, the event will take place in Madrid, including a touristic train trip to Segovia. Not only this is a country which has nice warm weather and beautiful beaches, but its rail network system is considered one of the best in Europe.

Isn't it the best place to celebrate our 25th anniversary?

Location

The event will take place in Atocha Station (Madrid).
